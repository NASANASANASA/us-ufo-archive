NASA-UAP-D014, Mercury-Redstone 4, July 21, 1961

Official U.S. government UAP archive record: NASA-UAP-D014, Mercury-Redstone 4, July 21, 1961. Agency: NASA. Incident Date: 7/21/61. Incident Location: North Atlantic Ocean.

Structured Summary

This record is a AUD UAP-related asset released by NASA in Release 02. The incident date is listed as 7/21/61, and the incident location is listed as North Atlantic Ocean. This page preserves the official source link, original description, and structured metadata.

Official Description

During the recovery of the fourth launch and second crewed spaceflight of Project Mercury, Mercury-Redstone 4 (MR-4) Liberty Bell 7, the recovery team discusses a dye pack in the water that did not activate.
