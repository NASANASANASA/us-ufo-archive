NASA-UAP-D016, Preliminary Gemini 4 Crew Debriefing, Part I, 1965

Official U.S. government UAP archive record: NASA-UAP-D016, Preliminary Gemini 4 Crew Debriefing, Part I, 1965. Agency: NASA. Incident Date: 6/9/65. Incident Location: North Atlantic Ocean.

Structured Summary

This record is a PDF UAP-related asset released by NASA in Release 03. The incident date is listed as 6/9/65, and the incident location is listed as North Atlantic Ocean. This page preserves the official source link, original description, and structured metadata.

Official Description

This document is a preliminary transcript (Part I) derived from voice recordings of the Gemini 4 flight crew debriefing taken aboard the recovery ship, USS Wasp, on June 9, 1965. Astronaut Ed White recounts seeing “sparkles” during the flight.
