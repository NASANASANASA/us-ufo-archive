DOW-UAP-PR022, Unresolved UAP Report, Syria, July 2022

Registro del archivo público UAP del Gobierno de Estados Unidos: DOW-UAP-PR022, Unresolved UAP Report, Syria, July 2022. Agencia: Departamento de Guerra. Fecha del incidente: N/A. Lugar del incidente: Syria.

Resumen estructurado

Este registro es un material relacionado con UAP en formato VID, publicado por Departamento de Guerra en Publicación 01. La fecha del incidente figura como N/A y el lugar como Syria. La página conserva el enlace a la fuente oficial, la descripción original y los metadatos principales.

Descripción oficial en inglés

The United States Central Command submitted a report of an unidentified anomalous phenomenon (UAP) to the All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office (AARO) consisting of 14 seconds of video footage from an infrared (left) and electro-optical (right) sensor aboard a U.S. military platform in 2022. An accompanying mission report, DoW-UAP-D16, described the UAP as “moving from north to south.”

Video Description: At the five second mark, the video depicts an object moving from right to left across the top right quarter of the sensor field-of-view.

This video description is provided for informational purposes only. Readers should not interpret any part of this description as reflecting an analytical judgment, investigative conclusion, or factual determination regarding the described event’s validity, nature, or significance.
